New Content For Rouge Company, SMITE, and Paladins Shown at Hi-Rez Showcase

New Content For Rouge Company, SMITE, and Paladins Shown at Hi-Rez Showcase

1.8.2021

By Hi-Rez Studios

New Seasons await players of Rogue Company, SMITE, and Paladins -- and Hi-Rez Studios unveiled all the epic details at our annual keynote today.


Read on for all the details of the bombastic new Rogue Kestral, a new Babylonian Conquest Map and Gods, and the Cosmic Horror Champion Yagorath.

Next month, Kestrel, our newest Rogue, jumpstarts the first season of Year One in Rogue Company. Kestrel splits her time between saving the world from imminent destruction with Rogue Company and running a multinational business empire. Kestrel brings her advanced assault rifle, SMG, and a set of Halo Drones to the battlefield and has a reputation for winning any battle she engages in, whether it’s on the battlefield or in the boardroom!


Seven additional Rogues will accompany Kestrel throughout 2021, giving players a diverse lineup that will cover a range of playstyles. Don’t miss a single Rogue with our new Year One Pass, coming next month. The Year One Pass will grant immediate access to every Rogue released throughout the year - plus bonus content like Weapon Wraps and an exclusive Outfit.


Seasonal Rogue Company Battle Pass events are also coming throughout Year One, which has been our most requested community request. This first Battle Pass includes a bevy of rewards such as new Outfits for Fixer, Dallas, and Trench, and it even features our first ever Legendary Scorch Outfit - as a rebellious cyber-demon ready to turn her enemies into a pile of ash!


Year One will also have players starting gunfights across the globe as we introduce new maps and new modes during each season. Our first new map will be The Hollows, a secretive location built by the mysterious Jackal organization with the intent of launching a rocket to the stars. The next update will also debut our new 2v2 Wingman mode, which sees players engaging in tense battles across all-new Skirmish maps.

SMITE Season 8: Dawn of Babylon arrives with a new map for Conquest mode and an all-new Babylonian pantheon of Gods.


Premiering on Jan. 26th, SMITE Season 8 will overhaul our Conquest map with new decor inspired by Baylonian myth. Cthulhu has been banished by the monster goddess Tiamat and the battlegrounds have been purged of his dark influence. However, it has now been remade in Tiamat’s image, complete with Babylonian architecture and brilliant gold ornamentation. This new Conquest map is also larger and features new Jungle challenges, offering opportunities for players to develop new strategies.


The best part? This year’s Conquest map is a living map and will evolve with quarterly updates featuring new visuals and gameplay changes. There’s always something new to look forward to on the Battleground of the Gods in Season 8.


However, Tiamat hasn’t just reshaped the battlegrounds. She’s also SMITE’s next playable God, adding the brand-new Babylonian Pantheon to SMITE. Following soon thereafter, the highly anticipated epic hero Gilgamesh will arrive to challenge the monster goddess’ reign. Both Gods will be added to SMITE’s roster in early 2021 with Tiamat arriving next month!

Paladins will launch Season 4: Calamity on Feb. 3rd alongside the all-devouring worm Yagorath.


Yagorath, a world-consuming entity of the Maw, comes to the Realm after being teased for years. Starting with the shattering of the moon and the creation of Io, and culminating with her release by the corrupted former Seal Guardian, Vora, Yagorath is a powerful threat to the entire Realm of Paladins.


2021 is going to be an amazing year for all Hi-Rez titles, and we can’t wait to share everything that is on the horizon with you. Thank you to all our players that support our titles - if it wasn’t for you, we wouldn’t be able to make these games we all hold so dear. Whether you are a fan of the tactical shootouts in Rogue Company, the epic skirmishes of SMITE, or the fast-paced action of Paladins, we will see you all in-game during our incredible new seasons!
